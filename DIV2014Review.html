A word from the man behind the curtain:

As we head into year 8 for DIV we are making plans to continue with our tradition of just kicking ass. With space, entry and some other small concerns addressed for this year, we’re ready to rock. Make sure to get yourself to Oshkosh, WI on 7/19/14 and join us as we celebrate the scene we know and love.

We’re keeping with the later in the day time frame, and will be moving to the third floor of the parking structure. Once again, we’ll also be welcoming our brothers and sisters from other facets of car culture, this year with a dedicated exhibition area. Of course Beckets Restaurant will be our ever gracious hosts providing the eats and drinks on the ramp.

The most important thing to note is the change in arrival/roll-in time. This year pre-registered cars are asked to begin arriving between 1 & 2PM. Non pre-registered cars can start arriving between 2 & 3PM. By staggering the categories we can alleviate confusion at the gate for the registration people and reduce the amount of time spent waiting to enter. Also, should space become an issue again, the folks who took the time to pre-register will have a guaranteed spot in a preferred area. As always, judging will begin at 4:00PM.

This will be, by far, the most baddest assed DIV to date. With growth, spectacular people in our scene, kick ass steel, dope beats, a slick venue… Honestly, what more could you ask for?

So, go get yourselves registered. And make sure to hit up Scooters, Eurohanger, Madfurther, Dubs Downtown & NoWo as well. #scenerep
