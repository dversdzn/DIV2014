Show Info…

This years show will be held on Saturday, July 19th, in downtown Oshkosh. Gates open at 1:00 PM., show starts at 3:30ish and all cars must be in place by 4 p.m. in order to be judged.

Friday night there will be a Pre-Show Party/GTG at Bergstrom VW. We're still working on the details, so keep checking back for updates.

Saturday night there will be Post Show craziness at a location to be disclosed later. DJ’s, Ballers and Beats! Good times to be had. So make sure to stick around and take in a little of what Oshkosh has to offer.

Lodging…

So as many of you have heard, the big hotel next door has been completely rehabbed and is now super fancy. Unfortunately, because of this, they no longer want to be our friend so they're not working any deals for rooms. Meh, who needs ‘em?

Unfortunately, it seems that the trend is to no longer give any kind of deal on room rates because none of the local hotels want to play ball

The only folks that are really being gracious and extending an invitation are the folks over at the Gruenhagen Conference Center. While not the Ritz, these are great dormitory style rooms for the budget minded. For $35/night you get a nice, clean room that's only a few blocks from the show. I know those who have stayed here over the last few years have been pretty pleased with what they got for the money. Give them a call, make sure you mention Dubs In the Valley. Rate is good for both Friday and Saturday night.
