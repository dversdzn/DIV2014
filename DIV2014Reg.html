Pre-Registration is officially OPEN!!!

Day of registration will be available for $25 but WILL NOT include a show shirt. Shirts will, however, be available for purchase for an additional $15 but will only be available on a first come, first serve basis. And quantities are limited. So get there early is you slacked on pre-reg and want a shirt.

Pre-registration is $25 and includes entry to the show on Saturday, a show shirt, and a bunch of other cool schwag. Pre-registration will close at midnight on Sunday, July 6th.

In an effort to help expedite the check-in process, please make sure to print a copy of your registration receipt and bring it with you the day of the show.

Show Field & Exhibition Classes

So as DIV continues to grow, we have decided to bring back the exhibition class. This class is open to all makes/models/vehicles. Basically, if it has a motor in it, it's welcome. So if you want to show your ride but it's not a VW or Audi, don't worry, go right ahead and register for the Exhibition Class and we'll get ya taken care of.

We're always trying to come up with new ways to help improve the show and everyone's experience, so this year we're doing something new for the Show Field, we're doing away with individual classes. So if you're in a VW or an Audi, just register for "Show Registration" and you'll be in the main show field.
